About Ryhgo Narita

Ryhgo Narita is a Japanese light novelist. His most famous work are Baccano! and Durarara!!, which are all made into Animation Series later on.
Narita's works feature an ensemble cast of characters with their own stories. The characters' life intersect with each other in unexpected ways. His work, unlike many other authors, do not have a theme to express in particular. The characters he create also envolves many supernatural forces and exaggerated emotions or conflicts, which increase the theaticality in the story.
